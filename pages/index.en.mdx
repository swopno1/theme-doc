# Welcome

Welcome to [AmirNFT's](https://www.amirnft.com/) theme documentation site. It is the official documentation site for [AmirNFT's](https://www.amirnft.com/). You can find all information about how to edit or update the [AmirNFT's theme](https://www.amirnft.com/theme) on this site.

As you know, we have a bunch of themes and addons for different platforms. You can chose/purchase any of them and then easily modify with the guideline of this site.

### Where to start?

We'll try to make things simple and clear to avoid any confusion. But we also try to explain things as details as possible. Pleas select your theme from the [theme list](http://localhost:3002/edit-theme) and follow the instruction.
